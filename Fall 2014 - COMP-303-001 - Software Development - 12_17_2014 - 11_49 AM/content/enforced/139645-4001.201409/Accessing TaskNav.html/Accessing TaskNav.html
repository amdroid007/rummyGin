Getting Started

To get unlimited access to TaskNav for the duration of the term, follow these instructions:

  1. Fill in the entrance survey.
  2. If you want to participate in the study, read, print, and sign the consent form. Then take a picture or scan of page 2. If you choose to participate, thank you.
  3. Email christoph.treude@mail.mcgill.ca the survey and (if applicable) the consent form. The information will be kept in confidentiality by Dr. Treude until the final COMP 303 grades have been submitted.
  4. You will shortly thereafter receive your account information. By using TaskNav you agree not to share your account.
  5. Got to http://tasknav.cs.mcgill.ca and search away!

What to expect

At first, only the "normal" text-search feature will be activated. This will be part of a warm-up phase to let you get used to the tool. Once a sufficient number of queries have been received, we will enable the powerful auto-complete and task-based querying features.

If you have problems

For general questions, you can post a message to the TaskNav forum.

If you think you found a problem with TaskNav, go to our bugzilla database and file a bug report (you will need to create an account if you don't have one). This will also give you real-life experience with bug reporting. Don't forget to read the bug authoring guidelines and make sure your bug has not already been reported. The text above the bug-reporting form will point you to some instructions.
